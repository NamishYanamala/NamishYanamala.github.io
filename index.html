Hey, I'm Namish — a Computer Science student who got tired of just using security tools and wanted to understand (and build) the machinery behind them.

Lately I've been obsessed with the overlap between offensive security and AI agents: what happens when you give an LLM tools, a scope, and a target? My goal is to build systems that think like an attacker so defenders can stay ahead of one.

When I'm not breaking my own lab environment, I'm learning new attack techniques, reading CVEs, and figuring out how autonomous agents should (and shouldn't) be trusted with dangerous tools.
